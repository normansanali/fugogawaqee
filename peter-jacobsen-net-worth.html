What is Peter Jacobsen's Net Worth?

Peter Jacobsen is an American professional golfer and commentator who has a net worth of $6 million. Peter Jacobsen was born in Portland, Oregon in March 1954. Jacobsen played golf at the University of Oregon and turned professional after winning the Oregon Open as an amateur. Jacobsen is known as one of golf's most colorful personalities. He was formerly part of the PGA Tour and is now a part of the Champions Tour. He won seven championships on the PGA Tour including the Buick-Goodwrench Open, Colonial National invitation, Sammy Davis Jr.-Greater Hartford Open, Bob Hope Chrysler Classic, AT&T Pebble Beach National Pro-Am, Buick Invitational of California, and Greater Hartford Open.

As a part of the Champions Tour Jacobsen won the U.S. Senior Open in 2004 and the Ford Senior Players Championship in 2005. His best finish in the Masters Tournament was 11th in 1981 while his best finish in the U.S. Open was 7th in 1984. He also finished 11th at The Open Championship in 1985 and 3rd in the PGA Championship in 1983 and 1986. Peter won the PGA Tour Comeback Player of the Year in 2003. He won the Old Tom Morris Award in 2012 and the Payne Stewart Award in 2013. He owns the event management company Peter Jacobsen Sports.
