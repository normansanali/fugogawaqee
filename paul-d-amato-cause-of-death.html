 FAQs

What was Paul D’Amato’s cause of death?

Paul D’Amato died after a four-year battle with progressive supranuclear palsy, a rare brain disease that affects movement, balance, speech, and vision.

How did Paul D’Amato influence Wolverine’s character in Marvel Comics?

According to comic book writer and artist John Byrne, he based the look of Wolverine, a popular superhero in the X-Men series, on Paul D’Amato’s appearance in Slap Shot. He said, “He just had the look. He had crazy eyes. And that’s what Wolverine needs to have.”

What characters did Paul D’Amato play in Law & Order?

Paul D’Amato appeared in several episodes of Law & Order and its spin-off series, Law & Order: Criminal Intent. He played different characters in each episode, such as Delbert Kort, Art Lydell, Ralph Pena, Nesci, and Dom Lovino.

Who was Paul D’Amato’s partner at the time of his death?

Paul D’Amato was engaged to Marina Re, who confirmed the news of his death and said, “He was the most wonderful, sweetest guy, he fought so hard against this horrendous disease.”
