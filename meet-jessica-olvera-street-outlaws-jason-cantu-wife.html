Being married to a man who lives and breathes all things automobile can be really frustrating and scary at times. But Street Outlaws star Jason Cantu and wife Jessica Olvera shared a beautiful relationship, which lasted for over 24 years.

So, who is Jason’s patient wife? Get to know about Jessica Olvera as this article continues.

Meet Jessica Olvera, Street Outlaws Jason Cantu Wife

You might be surprised but Jessica Olvera and Jason Cantu weren’t even engaged until 2021. Yes! It was only on Valentine’s Day 2021 that the Street Outlaws star proposed to Jessica with a beautiful diamond ring which he flaunted over his FB.

At the time, he wrote, “Happy Valentine’s Day to my sweetheart Jessica Olvera… Love you. Even though we’ve been together for 23 years, we’ve always seen ourselves as a married couple. I think it’s time we make it official… She was surprised.”

The duo officially began their relationship on April 24, 1998.

Multiple sites even mentioned that the couple shares two kids — which isn’t true. The baby, Justin Robert, many might have confused as Jessica’s kid, is her nephew.

However, the duo does share a few dogs that they labeled as their “babies.”

According to Jessica, she felt “lucky to have found” Jason and hoped to spend the rest of her life with him.

Likewise, Jason is grateful to have met someone who also shared a passion for the automobile.

As of 2022, Jason rode a heavily modified Chevrolet S-10 packed with over 1,500 horsepower named  “La Vicktoria”. His truck was also considered one of Texas’ top No Prep small tire vehicles. But when he wasn’t racing, Jason worked as a mechanic

Reportedly, he pocked a handsome salary of $20,000 to $30,000 per episode on Street Outlaws. But then again considering the risk of street racing, no money would be enough to put one’s life on the line.

Back when Jason raced Jackie Knox Sr at Edinburg Motorsports Park, his truck climbed the railing before flipping multiple times. Luckily, Jason escaped the incident unscathed.

But Jason’s most viral moments on the show would certainly be his scuffle with fellow star Brian “Chucky” Davis.

Jessica Olvera Age

Jessica Olvera was 42 years of age in 2022.

She is the same age as her husband Jason.

Jessica Olvera Job

Jessica Olvera is a design professional.

A design professional includes the Architect, the Architect’s sub-consultants, and the design consultants engaged directly by the Design-Builder or Project Co.

Unfortunately, we do not exactly know what her job was.

But we’ve spotted Jessica quite a few times behind the screen of Street Outlaws. So, she must have worked with the show’s production team.

In Feb 2018, Jessica even posted an FB pic from her “special job” on Street Outlaws captioned, “Yesterday’s races…I felt so important with the special job I was assigned…had so much fun being behind the scenes!”

On her LinkedIn, she also mentioned herself to be a “provider” ever since 2010.

When Jessica wasn’t working, she was often seen reading books. In fact, Jessica is a book fanatic, who rarely missed book conferences. So far, she’s been to Book Bonanza 2018, TLA Dallas, and TLA 2019 Austin, TX.

“Another conference in the books! Nothing better than a conference where you can meet authors and be surrounded by books!!” Jessica once explained her passion.

Is Jessica Olvera On IG, Facebook?

No, as of April 2022, Jessica wasn’t on Instagram.

However, you can find her on Facebook at @jessica.olvera.39.

Most of her posts then featured her family.

Jessica Olvera Family

Jessica Olvera comes from a family of four.

Unfortunately, her parents are divorced. And as of 2022, her mother Letty Olvera lived in Brownsville, Texas, working as a sales associate at Lacks Furniture.

According to Jessica, her mother was a strong woman who raised her to be one as well. She confessed that she saw her mother go through some really hard times and she would cry herself to sleep. But the next morning, she would wake up stronger and even more determined than before.

As for Jessica’s siblings, she has a brother named Jesse Robert Olvera who was in the U.S. Army.

Jesse was born on Feb 27, 1987, and on his 31st birthday, Jessica took it to her FB to share, “I’m so grateful God chose you as my brother! “I love you Cabron” and know that I will always have your back no matter what! I hope you have a great day!”
