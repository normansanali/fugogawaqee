Savage Beauty Season 2 Cast: Who will be in?

While the casting lineup for the show’s second season is still under wraps, we know that the talented ensemble from the first season will be reprising their roles. The inaugural season featured an exceptional cast, with Rosemary Zimu at the forefront, brilliantly portraying the enigmatic Zinhl, the series’ protagonist. The first season also consisted of Dumisani Mbebe as Don Bhengu, Nthati Moshesh as Grace Bhengu, Angela Sithole as Thando Bhengu, Jesse Suntele as Phila Bhengu, Nambitha Ben-Mazwi as Linda Bhengu, Oros Mampofu as Ndu Bhengu, and Mpho Sebeng as Bonga. With such a stellar lineup, the anticipation for the return of these characters in the second season is palpable.
