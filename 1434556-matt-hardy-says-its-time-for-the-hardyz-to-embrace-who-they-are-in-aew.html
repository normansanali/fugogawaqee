Matt Hardy Says Its Time For The Hardyz To Embrace Who They Are In AEW

September 21, 2024
blog

Matt Hardy teases a big change for him and Jeff Hardy in All Elite Wrestling.

Last night on AEW Rampage, The Hardyz teamed with Mark Briscoe and picked up a trios win over Kip Sabian, Butcher, and The Blade. Backstage following the match, Matt and Jeff were confronted by Private Party, and The Hardyz were presented with a hint of a heel edge to them.

Matt Hardy later took to social media to comment on the segment. Hardy said it’s time for him and Jeff to embrace who they are in All Elite Wrestling, tweeting out:

“It’s time for us to accept & embrace who we really are… #LivingLegends Tag Team GOATs The Greatest in all of Space & Time The Fn Hardys!!! #AEWRampage Being humble & giving back hasn’t gotten us shit in @AEW. NO MORE, @JEFFHARDYBRAND,” Matt Hardy said in a post.

It’ll be interesting to see what happens when The Hardyz step in the ring with Private Party soon.
