Ewen Bremner is a Scottish actor. His acting career started in the 1980s. After some roles in movies, he became known for starring as Daniel “Spud” Murphy in Trainspotting in 1996. In the 2000s, he had notable roles in Pearl Harbor, Black Hawk Down, The Reckoning, and Alien vs. Predator. He portrayed King James VI on the TV series Elizabeth I. In the 2010s, he continued his film career with performances in You Will Meet a Tall Dark Stranger, Great Expectations, Jack the Giant Slayer, and A Poet in New York. He’s recently reprised his role as Spud in T2 Trainspotting. He has also played supporting role Charlie in Wonder Woman.
