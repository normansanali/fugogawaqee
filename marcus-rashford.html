Who is Marcus Rashford?

Marcus Rashford is a professional footballer from England. Marcus Rashford is a Manchester United Premier League striker and England national team player.

He became the first English player to score in a senior international match at the age of 21.

Where was Marcus Rashford born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Rashford was born in Wythenshawe, England, in the United Kingdom, on October 31, 1997. Melanie and Robert Rashford (mother and father) are both English.

He was born in the United Kingdom to Afro-English parents. Dane Rashford and Dwaine Maynard are his two siblings. Marcus spent his childhood in his hometown playing football.

Marcus began playing football with Fletcher Moss Rangers at the age of five. When he was seven years old, he joined the Manchester United Academy. From 2005 until 2015, he was a part of the United youth team.

In November 2015, Marcus was promoted to the senior team. He finished his schooling at Ashton-on-Mersey School.

How did Marcus Rashford start his Professional Career?
