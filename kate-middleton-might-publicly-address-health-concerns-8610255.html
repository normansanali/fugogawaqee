Kate Middleton Plans to Publicly Address Her Health

August 19, 2024
blog

To say concerns over Kate Middleton's health are out of control, would be a great understatement. With each day that passes without an update from the Palace, a new internet conspiracy theory emerges and the speculation about the Princess of Wales's well-being has only intensified since the release of that doctored Mother's Day photo.

And while Middleton initially hoped to keep the details of her planned abdominal surgery private, she now might speak out about her health and recovery when she returns to public duty, according to several sources at The Sunday Times.  

“They are at their most open when out interacting with members of the public and I can see a world in which the princess might discuss her recovery out on engagements," one insider told the outlet. "If she was going to do it, that’s how she would do it.” 

However, if she does address her health, she will do it on her terms. A friend of the princess added, “They will want to be clear and more open, but they’ll do it when they feel ready. I would expect that to be her instinct and it will be her call. They’re not going to be rushed.”

Elsewhere in the article, The Times's royal editor Roya Nikkhah wrote that Middleton and Prince William have been "shaken" by all the rumors. “It’s not that they (Kate and William) didn’t think there would be lots of speculation and interest, but they had confidence that people would give them the space they asked for, which they did for about a month.”

As previously noted by Kensington Palace, the Princess of Wales is expected to make her return to royal duty after Easter, which is still two weeks away. So, until then, expect the rumors to continue to run wild.
