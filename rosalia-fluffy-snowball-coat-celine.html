Rosala Wears a Fluffy Celine Coat Straight Off The Runway

August 21, 2024
blog

Rosalía is no stranger to head-turning fashion, whether she’s wearing it or watching it debut from the front row at Dior. Her style combines pieces that are exclusive and experimental—or in the case of her latest look, both.

For her Friday night appearance on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, Rosalía stepped out in multiple Celine pieces fresh off the runway. Wearing the Triomphe Boutis top and miniskirt, the singer once again added her own twist to the preppy “office siren” trend. Rather than donning the matching ivory set solo and spotlighting its gold buttons, Rosalía paired it with a fluffy circular coat that would make any Yeti envious.

Gotham

Dubbed the Confetti Coat, the white outerwear also hails from Celine’s Fall/Winter 2024 collection. Designed by Hedi Slimane—the same name behind a similar Yves Saint Laurent heart coat famously worn by Rihanna—the jacket’s fuzzy appearance comes courtesy of cashmere goat shearling (despite its cotton ball-like look). The material envelops its wearer, save for two small arm holes.

Keeping the spotlight on the coat, Rosalía opted for simple accessories. The singer channeled her Motomami aesthetic with the help of Dua Lipa’s signature stylist, Lorenzo Posocco, who dressed her in sheer black tights, white heels, and sharp wraparound sunglasses from Gentle Monster’s collaboration with BLACKPINK star Jennie. For glam, Rosalía stuck to a classic nude lip and left her hair down in a somewhat wet, slightly crimped ’do.

Outside of press shows, the Grammy winner has been busy traveling from city to city with her new beau, The Bear star Jeremy Allen White. Seen together in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago—in coordinating outfits, no less—the couple can’t seem to get enough of each other.
