Cora Miao (simplified Chinese: 缪骞人 ; traditional Chinese: 繆騫人 ; pinyin: Miào Qiānrén ; Jyutping: Miu6 Hin1jan4 ) is a Chinese actress who worked predominantly in Hong Kong films. During her career she was nominated for four Hong Kong Film Awards and four Golden Horse Film Festival awards, winning one. She won the Miss Photogenic award in the Miss Hong Kong Pageant in 1976 after graduating from the United States. She is married to film director Wayne Wang.
