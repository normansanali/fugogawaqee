    Chad Lindberg Career

    Chad starts his acting career as Rory in Black Circle Boys at the year 1997 Sundance Film Festival, from there, Chad made several guest appearances on popular television shows, such as ER, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and The X-Files. Lindberg continued pursuing films roles, particularly as Sherman O’Dell in October Sky as well as Jesse, a stuttering mechanic, in The Fast and the Furious.

    Chad received attention for his role in I Spit on Your Grave, the 2010 remake of the ’70s cult classic. Chad has also appeared in the recurring role of Chad Willingham on CSI: NY and as Ash on Supernatural.

    Chad took part in Tony Zierra’s 2011 documentary My Big Break, which follows the early careers of Lindberg, Wes Bentley, Brad Rowe along Greg Fawcett.

    Chad was also a guest star in an episode of The Cape as Hicks, a professional sniper assassin. Chad was more recently featured as a paranormal investigator on the Destination America program Ghost Stalkers.
