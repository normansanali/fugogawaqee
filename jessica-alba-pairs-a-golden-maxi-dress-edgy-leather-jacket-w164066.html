Jessica Alba Pairs a Golden Maxi Dress, Edgy Leather Jacket

August 15, 2024
blog

Edgy and glam, all in one look! Jessica Alba glowed in a gold Saint Laurent maxi dress while attending the design house’s L.A. fashion show at the Hollywood Palladium theater on Wednesday, February 10.

The Honest Company cofounder, 34, dressed the metallic number down with an edgy leather moto jacket and accessorized with a black crossbody bag. Her toned legs and strappy black sandals peeked through the center slit of the dress. Jacquie Aiche and Logan Hollowell rings added a little extra sparkle.

Alba matched the tough but polished balance of her outfit to her beauty, rocking a statement berry lip with bed-head hair, clipped to one side.

The A-list event, which was an ode to Hollywood music, was attended by Justin Bieber, Lady Gaga, Demi Moore and others, but the celeb who made Alba starstruck was Jane Fonda. She shared a pic of the Grace and Frankie star’s fireworks-inspired pantsuit on the red carpet via Instagram, writing, “Tonight @ysl show #janefonda -j’adore #SaintLaurent #lanights.”
