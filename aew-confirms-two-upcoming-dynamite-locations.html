AEW Confirms Two Upcoming Dynamite Locations

July 22, 2024
blog

AEW Dynamite is making some big plans for 2020. California fans still have to wait for live Dynamite, but Matt Jackson hinted that they might have plans for the spring.

The company revealed two more upcoming Dynamite locations today. Huntsville, Alabama will host Dynamite on February 5th at the Von Braun Center.

They will be going back to Texas on February 12th for an episode of Dynamite in Austin at the H.E.B Center at Cedar Park. Several AEW stars were listed on both promotional graphics for the events, but that doesn’t guarantee who will be on the shows.

Tickets for both events go on sale this Friday, December 13th.
