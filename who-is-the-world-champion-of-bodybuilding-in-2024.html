Who is the World Champion of Bodybuilding in 2024?

September 08, 2024

When it comes to bodybuilding, there are only a few names as huge as Chris Bumstead, a well-known name in the sport with an unmatched success. For Bumstead, becoming the 2024 Mr. World has been a journey of pure dedication, sheer hard-work and the quest for excellence.

Courtesy of Fan Arch, here is the full in-depth article on the background, accomplishments, workouts, diets, and journey of the current champion, giving an explicit & statistical data and informative context to the champion of Bodybuilding world.

Early Life and Background

Chris Jonathan Bumstead, born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada on February 2, 1995 It was his sisters boyfriend, Iain Valliere, who fired up his passion for the sport, and who not only put him on the stage, but coached him through it.

His upbringing, split between the UK and USA was one of incessantly driven progression, playing sports, he saw first hand that true greatness will always demand more, it requires drilling down to get that little bit more and, Bumstead knew what it took.

Professional Career

Bumstead, then 21 years old at the time, qualified for his pro card by winning the IFBB North American Bodybuilding Championship in 2016.

He instantly became a dominating figure in the Classic Physique division, winning Mr. Olympias back-to-back between 2019 and 2022.

As Bumstead's legend grows in the sport, so does his stranglehold on the Classic Physique division.

Competition History and Achievements

With a number of wins under his belt, such as the Classic Physique Olympia and the Toronto Pro, IFBB Pittsburgh Pro, Bumstead has a very solid competition record.

Naturally, winning regularly in Mr. Olympia Classic Physique contest has earned him a lot of prize money, which, based on his undeniable excellence and dominance in the sport, he well and truly deserves.

 

Training Regimen

Bumstead built the body that made him a champion with the help of his remarkable genetic potential and an extraordinary work ethic.

He trains using a mix of heavy lifting, hypertrophy focused exercises, compound movements and isolation exercises to target individual muscle groups.

Consistent training, intense focus and the ability to listen to his body has been key for the transformation from his early years to the now supercharged body he has, taking his performance and appearance to the next level.

Diet and Nutritional Strategy

Bumstead adheres to a diet that is detailed and has six meals a day for a goal of 500g of protein, 90g of fats, and 800g carbohydrates in the competitive season, with some slight alterations made overall time to help you accomplish your goals.

Although his off-season diet does relax a bit, the overall diet is in line with maintaining a healthy balance, as there's still room for treats and cheat meals.

Along with daily supplements like fat-burners, whey protein, testosterone, creatine, BCAAs and super greens blend supports his grueling workout regimen.

Challenges Faced

Bumstead's road to fitness icon status has come with difficulties.

Whether battling with injuries and dark mental health days or juggling the pressure of performing internationally whilst attempting to lead a normal life and handling financial difficulties, he epitomizes resilience and grit.

In spite of these obstacles, Bumstead's steadfast dedication to his vision of who he wanted to become has propelled him to become a household name in the bodybuilding world and has served as a source of motivation to athletes and fitness enthusiasts across the globe.

Business and Net Worth

In addition to his achievements in bodybuilding, Bumstead has proven his ability as an entrepreneur with Gymshark and Revive MD ventures.

This, along with an estimated net worth of approximately $5-6 million shows just how passionate he is about fitness and business that once again cements his influence and reach in the industry.

All in all

Chris Bumstead's Road To The Top of The Bodybuilding Mountain is a testament to the human characteristics of hard work, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

His story is a great inspiration for anyone just starting out in the fitness industry up to the reigning world champion of bodybuilding in 2024.

From his early, humble beginnings in Canada to his rise as one of the best bodybuilders in the world, Bumstead's story is an familiar one for an athlete of any generation: a true testament to the concept of chasing personal best and the unyielding quest for greatness in the realm of bodybuilding.
