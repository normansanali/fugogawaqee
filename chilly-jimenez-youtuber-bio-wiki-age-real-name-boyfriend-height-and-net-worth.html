Professionally, Ladye Wanda Jimenez a.k.a Chilly Jimenez is an American YouTuber. She initially gained fame as a former partner of the YouTube gamer Logan Thirtyacre. Likewise, she also has a solo channel on YouTube titled ‘Chilly.’ She created the channel on April 15, 2008. She began with pet videos in which she featured her kitten.
