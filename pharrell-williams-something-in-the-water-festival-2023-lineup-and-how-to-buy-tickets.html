Pharrell Williams Something in the Water festival 2023 lineup and how to buy tickets

September 30, 2024

The Something in the Water festival will be coming up early next year, and tickets are set to go on sale quite soon. The festival will be headlined Pharrell Williams who founded the festival in 2019. The Something in the Water festival will run over the course of three days from April 28, 2023-April 30, 2023. It will take place in Virginia Beach, Virginia, and all ages are welcome at the festival with children under five getting in for free.
