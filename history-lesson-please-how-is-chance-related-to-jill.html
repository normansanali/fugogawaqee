Jill Foster, who was a maid for Catherine Chancellor, had an affair with Catherine’s husband Phillip Chancellor I. They had a son, Phillip Chancellor II. Phillip Chancellor II married Nina Webster (Christine’s best friend), and they had a child Phillip Chancellor III, who goes by the name of Chance. As you can deduce, Chance is Jill Foster Chancellor Abbot’s grandson.
