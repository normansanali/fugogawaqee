August 24, 2024

Netflix has just released a new trailer for Raising Dion season 2!

Alisha Wainwright and Ja’Siah Young return in the lead roles of Nicole and her son Dion.

Season two takes place two years after defeating the Crooked Man, played by Jason Ritter, following Dion as he develops his powers with his mom and Tevin (Rome Flynn), his Biona trainer who catches Nicole’s eye.

The series also stars Jazmyn Simon (Kat), Sammi Haney (Esperanza), Ali Ahn (Suzanne Wu), Gavin Munn (Jonathan King), Aubriana Davis (Janelle Carr), Tracey Bonner (Simone Carr) and Josh Ventura (David Marsh).

Season two is set to premiere on on Netflix on February 1, 2022. Check out the full trailer down below!
