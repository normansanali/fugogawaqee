    • This is the map of the Remnant Vault on the planet Voeld. You visit this location during Restoring a World mission. You should check this mission description for more information about this vault, its secrets, etc.

    Points of interest

    Below you will find some information about the points of interest located on "Remnant Vault on Voeld" map.

    It appears that the Voeld vault, unable to gravitationally compensate for Voeld's change in orbit, has triggered a controlled melt of frozen carbon dioxide in the permafrost. Projections show this will thicken the atmosphere, gradually raising Voeld's temperature to ease the ice age. This reveals that Remnant vaults are capable of sophisticated analysis and response to planetary conditions.

    You should check Restoring a World mission description for more information about this vault, its secrets, etc.

    Correct solution of Remnant decryption puzzle

    The Remnant decryption puzzle in this vault locks one of the consoles. Solving it allows you to gain Skill Up +2.
