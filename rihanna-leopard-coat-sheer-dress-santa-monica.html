Rihanna Wears a Leopard Coat and Sexy Sheer Dress in Santa Monica

August 10, 2024
blog

Rihanna is continuing to do what she does best: be effortlessly fabulous.

The Fenty Beauty and Savage x Fenty founder was photographed leaving a restaurant in Santa Monica last night, showing off a daring ensemble. For her dinner date, she wore a sheer, fiery-printed body-con midi dress paired with a leopard-print faux-fur coat, a red multi-print shoulder bag, layered silver necklaces, strappy black stilettos from Amina Muaddi, and a casual black baseball cap from buzzy brand R13.

BACKGRID

Rihanna's latest outing isn't the first time the star wore standout outerwear. Back in January while leaving Emilio's Ballato in New York City, RiRi rocked a brown faux-fur number, which she paired with a chartreuse-green blouse and distressed denim.

Though the singer is remaining mum on the whereabouts of her highly anticipated ninth studio album (aptly nicknamed R9 by her fans), she's been dedicated in the meantime to promoting her other business endeavors on social media.

Most recently, RiRi stunned in a new set of spring campaign images for her billion dollar lingerie company Savage x Fenty. In the images, Rihanna wore a retro-inspired, floral-print bra and panty set paired with a matching kimono-style robe.

She also showed off the latest limited-edition version of her coffee-table book, The Rihanna Book, now available to purchase.

"Only 500 of these GIANT sized versions of #theRIHANNAbook in the world!" wrote Rihanna alongside an image of her posing with the book poolside.
