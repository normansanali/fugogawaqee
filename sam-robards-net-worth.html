Sam Robards Wiki Biography

Sam Prideaux Robards is a New York City, New York-born American actor best known for his roles in movies like “A.I. Artificial Intelligence”,and “Life As A House”. Born on 16 December, 1961, Sam is also known for being the son of deceased actors Jason Robards, Jr., and Lauren Bacall. A successful actor, Sam has been active in the field of acting since 1980.

A popular name when it comes to acting, how rich is Sam Robards as of today? In 2015, Sam has been counting his net worth at $3 million. Obviously, the major source of his wealth is his ongoing successful career as an actor. His involvement in Hollywood movies, American television shows as well as Broadway and off-Broadway theater productions, have been helping raise his net worth for all these years.

Raised in New York City, Sam had a great inclination towards acting since his childhood as both his parents were successful actors in Hollywood. Sam attended Sarah Lawrence College and also studied theatrical skills in the National Theater Institute in Waterfront, Connecticut. In 1980, Sam initiated his acting career being a part of “Album”, an off-Broadway Production. After a series of dramas in theaters, he made his debut in Hollywood in the movie “Tempest”, after which Sam’s popularity as an actor began to rise as also did his net worth.

During his career, Sam has appeared in various Hollywood movies like “Mrs. Parker And The Vicious Circle”, “Fandango”, “Life As A House”, “The Art Of Getting By” and many more. In addition to this, he also got an opportunity to appear alongside his father, Jason Robards in the movie “Bright Lights, Big City” which was released in 1988. His collaboration with his mother, Lauren Bacall can be seen in the movie “Pret-a-Porter” which was released in 1993. Needless to say, all of these projects have been very significant in adding to Sam’s wealth.

Apart from Hollywood, Sam is also famous as a television actor. He is best known for his recurring roles in television series like “Get A Life”, “Gossip Girl” and “Trim”, but he has also appeared in television series including “The West Wing”, “Law & Order: Criminal Intent”, “Sex And The City”, “Body Of Proof” and many others. More recently, Sam has been appearing regularly on television in the cast of “Twisted”. All of these television projects have not only helped Sam in polishing his acting skills, but have also helped him add to his net worth. In recognition of his talent in acting, Sam was nominated for a Tony Award in 2002 for his role in the play “The Man Who Had All The Luck”.

As for his personal life, Sam has married twice and has three children. His first marriage was with actress Suzi Amis in 1985. The couple had a baby before they decided to divorce in 1993. Sam’s second marriage is with Sidsel Jensen, from 1997. The couple is now parents to their two sons. For now, Sam has been living his life as a successful actor while enjoying his present net worth of $3 million.
