Top 10 Influencers with the Biggest Instagram Followings in 2024

August 20, 2024
blog

In 2024, Instagram continues to be a powerful platform for influencers, with millions of users actively engaging with content from various personalities. Let's delve into the top 10 influencers with the biggest Instagram followings and explore their impact and following on the platform.

1. Instagram - 540M Followers

Instagram, the platform itself, holds the top position as the most followed account on Instagram. With an enormous 540 million followers, Instagram sets the benchmark for engagement and influence on the platform.

2. Cristiano Ronaldo - 540M Followers

Cristiano Ronaldo, the legendary footballer, stands as the biggest male influencer on Instagram and boasts an impressive 540 million followers. Known for his athletic prowess and engaging content, Ronaldo's posts can earn a staggering $1.6 million each, reaffirming his immense influence.

3. Kylie Jenner - 370M Followers

With over 370 million followers, Kylie Jenner has solidified her status as one of the most influential personalities on Instagram. As the highest-paid influencer in 2019, Jenner's estimated earnings of $1.2 million per post attest to her significant impact on the platform.

4. Leo Messi - 380M Followers

Lionel Messi, the renowned footballer, commands a massive following of over 380 million on Instagram. His global appeal and engaging content have contributed to his substantial influence on the platform.

5. Selena Gomez - 360M Followers

Selena Gomez, with over 360 million followers, is a prominent figure on Instagram. Notably, Gomez utilizes her platform to advocate for essential causes, such as mental health, resonating with millions of followers globally.

6. Dwayne Johnson - 350M Followers

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson's Instagram presence, with over 350 million followers, reflects his significant influence and engagement with fans. His diverse content and charismatic persona have established him as a formidable Instagram influencer.

7. Kim Kardashian - 250M Followers

Kim Kardashian's Instagram following surpasses 250 million, underscoring her substantial influence as a social media personality. Her impact on Instagram extends beyond her celebrity status, making her a key figure in the influencer landscape.

8. Ariana Grande - 240M Followers

Ariana Grande's Instagram following exceeds 240 million, cementing her status as a prominent influencer. With a strong presence in the music industry, Grande's influence extends to her captivating Instagram content.

9. Beyonce - 200M Followers

Beyonce's Instagram following has surpassed 200 million, positioning her as a significant influencer on the platform. Her influence transcends music, resonating with a vast audience worldwide.

10. Khloe Kardashian - 190M Followers

Khloe Kardashian, with over 190 million followers, rounds out the top 10 influencers on Instagram. Her impactful presence and engagement with followers underscore her influence as a prominent Instagram personality.

The top 10 influencers with the biggest Instagram followings in 2024 represent a diverse array of personalities, each wielding significant influence on the platform. Their massive followings and impactful content highlight the profound role of Instagram in shaping modern-day influence and engagement.
